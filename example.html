Does The Wrong Email Program Open When You Click "Send Email"?

You need to change your default email program to your preferred email program.

Microsoft Windows 11
  1. Open Settings.
  2. Click Apps.
  3. Click Default Apps.
  4. Search for your email program and click it.
  5. Click the button below MAILTO and click your email program.
Apple macOS
  1. Open the Mail app.
  2. From the Mail menu in the menu bar, choose Settings (or Preferences). You need to have already added an email account in Mail to change the settings.
  3. Click General.
  4. Choose an email app from the Default email reader pop-up menu.

For more help, see https://support.apple.com/en-au/HT201607
